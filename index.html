About Us

Three Star Food Products is a family-run business rooted in the heart of Palakkad, Kerala. With a passion for quality and tradition, we bring you the finest dry fruits, dried shrimp, and a wide variety of sweets & candys. Our commitment is to source only the freshest ingredients, ensuring every bite is a taste of authenticity and care.

We pride ourselves on hygiene, customer satisfaction, and affordable prices. Visit our store or order online to experience the flavors that have delighted generations!
